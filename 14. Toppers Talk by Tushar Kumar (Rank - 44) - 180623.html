DISCUSSION BY TUSHAR KUMAR (2:05 PM)

  • UPSC preparation is not your complete life, it is much beyond that
  • Should have clarity on why one opted for the preparation
  • Need to understand the demand of the examination
  • Do prepare your plan B
  • The process of preparation should have a proper balance of strategy for examination and non-academic aspects like social, skill, and hobbies
  • Opt for limited sources for ease of revision
  • Develop the art of answer writing within the given time frame
  • The decision of the optional subject should be taken on the basis of past years' trends and one's aptitude towards the subject
  • Preparation of optional shall be done by going through the syllabus, toppers' strategy and PYQs
